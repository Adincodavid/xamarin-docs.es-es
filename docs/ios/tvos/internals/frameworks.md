* AudioUnit

* Foundation
* GameController
* GameKit

* MediaAccessibility
* MediaPlayer

* MetalKit

* OpenGES
* SceneKit
* Seguridad
* SIMD

* SystemConfiguration
* TVMLKit (agregado)
* TVServices (agregado)
* UIKit

## <a name="unsupported-frameworks"></a>Marcos de trabajo no admitidos

* Libreta de direcciones
* AddressBookUI
* AssetsLibrary
* Contactos
* ContactsUI
* CoreAudioKit
* CoreMIDI
* CoreMotion
* CoreTelephony
* EventKit

* ExternalAccessory

* HomeKit

* LocalAuthentication
* MapKit
* MessageUI

* NetworkExtension
* NewsstandKit
* NotificationCenter
* PassKit
* Fotos
* PhotosUI
* PushKit
* QuickLook
* ReplayKit
* SafariServices
* Social
* Twitter
* VideoToolbox
* WatchConnectivity
* WatchKit
* WebKit
